# Global Settings Tab:     Forced Comments Sub-Tab 
---

To open the Forced Comments sub-tab of the 
[Global Settings tab](<globset.md>), start by selecting the Global Settings menu item from the Administrator menu on the
[main 
form](<7jjr.md>).
